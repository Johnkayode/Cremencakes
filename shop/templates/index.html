About Creme n' Cakes

Rebranding the cake culture..

The Creme n' Cakes Bakeries started off in 2018 by Mrs. Omoni Craig with its main branch in Allen Avenue, Ikeja. Over the years, Creme n' Cakes has grown larger with over 10 branches in Lagos and Abuja.
Our aim at Creme n' Cakes is to deliver the best of cake delicacies to your footstep.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge for specialty, sculpted or novelty cakes?

Prices vary depending on size and complexity of the design. For specialty or novelty cakes, please contact us on WhatsApp for a price quote.

Where do you deliver your cakes, cookies or cupcakes

We do offer delivery services pending availability. We generally deliver to areas within Lagos and Abuja only. The delivery charges vary based on the location we are delivering to.

Your cakes are beautiful, but do they taste good too?

At Creme N' Cakes, we make all our cakes, cookies, bread and pastries to order. Meaning, we're baking them when you order them. We only use fresh ingredients, including real butter, milk, flour, sugar and eggs! This means our cakes not only look beautiful, but they also taste delicious.

Can i supply my own cake design ideas?

Yes absolutely! We need to work together to incorporate your style and tastes into your order. We work individually with each client to ensure your cake, cupcakes or cookies are tailored for your event.

How far in advance should I place my order?

Orders should be placed at least one day in advance...but if you have a short notice order, ask us, and we'll try to get it done for you if possible. We ask that you give us at least 2 to 3 weeks notice on specialty cake orders.

Can I pick up my order at the store? Where are you located

Yes, you can always pick up your order within the opening hours of the store you are picking up from. We are located at:
1. 33, Allen Avenue, Ikeja, Lagos.
2. The Hive, Plot 16, Kuboye Street, Oniru, Lagos.
3. Creme n' Cakes, Circles Mall, Gwagalada, Abuja.
